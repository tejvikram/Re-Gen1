Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy

What is Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP)?

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) is a concentration of platelets derived from a sample of a patientâ€™s own blood. This concentration contains growth factors and proteins that are involved in the healing process. PRP therapy involves isolating this plasma from the blood and injecting it into areas of the body that require treatment.

Applications of PRP Therapy

PRP therapy is used in orthopedics, aesthetics, and more. It can help reduce inflammation, promote healing, and improve skin texture or hair density.

Procedure

The process involves drawing blood, separating platelets, and injecting the concentrated plasma into the treatment area. Recovery is quick, with minimal downtime.
