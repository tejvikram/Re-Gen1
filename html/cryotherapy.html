Cryotherapy

What is Cryotherapy?

Cryotherapy is a treatment method that uses extreme cold to address various medical and aesthetic conditions. It involves the application of cold temperatures locally or systematically to reduce inflammation, pain, and improve overall wellness.

Applications of Cryotherapy

Cryotherapy is widely used for injury recovery, inflammation reduction, and skin rejuvenation. Athletes often use whole-body cryotherapy to reduce muscle soreness and improve performance.

Procedure

The treatment involves localized application of cold compresses or entry into cryotherapy chambers for a few minutes at extremely low temperatures, depending on the type of treatment.
